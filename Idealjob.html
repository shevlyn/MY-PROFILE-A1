A description of the position, and particularly what makes this position appealing to you

The digital information officer role is working alongside a secret intelligence agency controlled by the government. Its core responsibility is to collect and protect digital records and intelligence related information to ensure security against transnational crimes that potentially harm on a larger national scale. This is certainly a job that I would apply for in the future as I have grown up watching crime investigations, I have always been curious in finding information from different sources to solve mystery and from my perspective this is a job with similar motive. This job would be filling two passions of mine, one for IT and the other working for a secret intelligence team.

A description of the skills, qualifications and experience required for the position.

In order to be a perfect candidate for this role there are many requirements to meet and more so because of the fact that it is a relatively important job for the protection of our country as it is performed under the government. To be successful you must hold a qualification in records or information management and/or have demonstrated experience in a similar role. Should have the ability to manage electronic records and information. They must demonstrate professionalism in customer service and communication skills both written and verbal - to provide research assistance. Additionally, maintaining a updated knowledge of policies, records and contributing to the development of digitalisation information is a bonus.
